Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

At Niwasi Mukbdhir Vidhyalaya, Gokunda, we focus on the all-round development of hearing-disabled students. Along with education, we organize cultural events, art competitions, sports, and festivals that help students build confidence and express their talents. Our dedicated teachers and staff work together to create a positive, inclusive, and joyful learning environment where every child feels valued and encouraged to grow.

Our Special Classrooms for Student

At our school, we have specially designed classrooms for hearing-impaired students to provide a comfortable, supportive, and effective learning environment. These classrooms are equipped with visual learning aids, interactive boards, soundproofing, and other modern tools to make lessons easier to follow.
